# institution/

The organization's formal, governing record — the documents that define what Turner
Strategic _is_ and how it operates, as distinct from what it builds (`apps/`,
`packages/`) or how it works day to day (`memory/`).

- **`constitution/`** — founding rules and governance documents: how decisions get
  made, who has authority over what.
- **`publications/`** — formal published output, intended for an audience beyond the
  organization itself.
- **`standards/`** — engineering and editorial standards that apply across the
  organization's work.
- **`offices/`** — definitions of roles and their responsibilities.

Empty as of the initial infrastructure scaffold — populated as the organization
formalizes each area.
